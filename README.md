# Velora-Fashion-E-commerce-App-UI-UX-Design
Velora Fashion E-commerce App UI UX Design is based on shopping users journey.

Before Velora

Users loves shopping online, but after placing an order she often feels uncertain. She doesn't know where her package is, can't easily contact the seller, and has to wait for updates. This lack of visibility makes the experience stressful.

After Velora

With Velora, Users can browse products freely, place an order securely, track its status in real time, and contact the seller directly through in-app chat. Instead of wondering what happens next, she stays informed and confident throughout the entire journey.
